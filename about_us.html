Our Story

As ASC Summer Intensive Students we were tasked with creating a project using the skills we developed throughout the program. We decided to create a coupon searching site to help those in need of finding coupons.

Our Mission

We know how hard it is to save money when it comes to buying groceries and necessities and so We are here to make it more affordable so you can save money.

Our Vision

Building a community of savvy shoppers who want to save money.
